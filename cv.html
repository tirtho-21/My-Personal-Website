How to Create Your Own Professional CV in Canva

Creating a professional CV is easy with Canva. Here's how:

  • 1
    Sign up for a free account at Canva.com
  • 2
    Search for "CV" or "Resume" in the templates section
  • 3
    Choose a template that matches your style
  • 4
    Customize it with your personal information, education, and work experience
  • 5
    Download your CV as a PDF file
  • 6
    Upload to Google Drive to get a shareable link for your website
